Wisps of Grief

dreaming

floating on the ocean
you come
only to go

in my dreams, I fear your death
in life, I live its breath

waking, I am stranded
sleeping, I am drowning

yet I wait
for the sun to set
so I can see you
if just for a minute
and make myself belief
that none of this is real
